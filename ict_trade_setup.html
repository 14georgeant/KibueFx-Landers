Market Structure and Price Action Insights from ICT’s “Elements of a Trade Setup”

Abstract

This paper analyzes key trading concepts from Michael J. Huddleston, widely known as the Inner Circle Trader (ICT), focusing on market structure and price action as taught in his video “Elements of a Trade Setup.” It breaks down the core logic behind smart money trading, specifically how traders can identify high-probability setups by recognizing liquidity pools, imbalances, and structural shifts.
